Datalist

Agrupa varios elementos <option>, una alternativa a <select>

Ejemplo

<label for="name">Choose a browser from this list:
    <inputlist ="browser" name ="myBrowser">
</label>
<datalist id="browsers">
    <optionvalue ="Chrome">
    <optionvalue ="Firefox">
    <optionvalue ="Internet Explorer (Muerto)">
    <optionvalue ="Opera">
    <optionvalue ="Safari">
    <optionvalue ="Microsoft Edge">
</datalist>
